Record Capture Screen

Tap anywhere on the screen to focus on that point.

Tap AF/MF to toggle manual focus.

Mark Screen

Tap and drag to scrub through the video's frames. Scrubbing is quadratic - the farther you drag the faster it flips through frames.

Tap the edges of the screen to manually flip to the next frame.

Capture List

If a capture is deleted, its associated mark metadata is deleted too.

The mark database can be retrieved from the device via iOS File Sharing. Plug the device into a Mac, click the device in Finder, select Is It Snappy?, and copy snappy-database.json to your computer.
